## 1. What is NoraCivilis?
NoraCivilis is a **Civilization Operating System** — a digital civilization with its own heart, memory, laws, engines, evolution, and governance.

It is built as a **multi-layer, distributed, self-healing, self-governing intelligence architecture** designed to grow, evolve, and operate as a complete civilization.

Products, services, gateways, and interactions.
